About DocNear

DocNear is a modern healthcare platform designed to bridge the gap between patients and quality medical professionals. Our mission is to make healthcare accessible, transparent, and convenient for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted digital bridge between patients and doctors, ensuring that quality healthcare is just a click away for every individual.

What We Offer

  • Search and discover verified doctors across multiple specialties
  • Real-time appointment booking with instant confirmation
  • Support for both Allopathy and Homeopathy branches
  • Interactive map view to find doctors near your location
  • Transparent doctor profiles with ratings and reviews

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of healthcare enthusiasts, technologists, and designers committed to transforming how people access medical care. Founded in 2024, DocNear has grown to serve thousands of patients across the country.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when booking appointments, including your name, contact details, and health preferences. We also collect usage data to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To communicate appointment confirmations and reminders
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To ensure the security of your account

Data Protection

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties without your consent. All medical data is handled in compliance with applicable healthcare data regulations.
